Vengeance

In Vengeance, you play the role of a former CIA field operative whose former partner and long time friend has died in a suspicious manner and suspects that there is something more to his friends death than what he's being told. He sets out to find out what really happend and will not stop until those who are responsible are dealt with.

Being a former CIA operative, you are extremely skilled at stealth, hand to hand fighting, and guerrilla combat. Even though you will be constantly out numbered and out gunned, with the skillset that you have at your disposal, you will always have a way to come out on top of any situation.

Your not the only one who wants to know the truth about your friend's death. There are others who work with you to help you through your missions. They can help by giving you intel and providing you with equipment and weapons.
