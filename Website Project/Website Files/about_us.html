About Us

How we started

We started our operations here at Danger Zone Game Studios in June of 2017. When we started, we only had 3 people on the team. Today we have 14. Even though we haven't been around very long, we have made big strides on several game projects.

Mission Statement

Our studios mission is to create games with the mindset of making games where player satisfaction and enjoyment are the highest priorities. We believe that there is an art to making video games and we have a strong passion for it.
