Road Rage

In Road Rage, you play the role of a professional race car driver who has been forced into illegal street racing because he accidently crossed a crime lord and the crime lord wants him to repay his "depts".

The rival street racers in Road Rage don't have the slightest bit of sportsmenship. You will have to be cautious around them because they are eager to get reckless and dangerous. It can range anywhere from trading paint to forcing you into other cars and obstacles.

You will get to choose and buy your cars from a large list of cars to race with. Anywhere from the low end tuners and muscle cars all the way to the high end exotics and super cars. You can also modify and soup up the cars that you already own. Keep in mind that you will have to be careful with your money becasue the crime lord could turn up at any time and demand money or collateral on the spot.
