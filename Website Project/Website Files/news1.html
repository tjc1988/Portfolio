Danger Zone Studio welcomes new team member

April - 12 - 2019

Today we are happy to announce that Nick Cricket is the newest member on the Danger Zone Game Studio team. Nick recently earned an Associates Degree in Game Development and also grew up in the same town that the Danger Zone game studio is located. Nick told us "I'm sort of lucky that you guys were right here where town. The only other option I had was to move away to a city that would be probably on either the east or west coast. It's nice to be able to at least get a start in this job field without having to move so far away".
