Hostiles

Hostiles is set in the year 2204. Humans have been colonizing other nearby planets for many years but have now encountered an coalition of alien races that want to destroy humanity for unknown reasons.

You play as a soldier that had been selected years before the current conflict to be one of the military elite that has been enhanced with bodily augmentations, put through the most rigorius and extreme training that has ever been implemented, and equipped with the latest weapons and technology that humanity has to offer.

In this game, you get to play in a variety of settings ranging from the outskirts, to the middle of Earth's largest citys, and even on board alien space ships and cruisers. You will get to use a broad selection of weapons and vehicles, both of human and alien origin.
