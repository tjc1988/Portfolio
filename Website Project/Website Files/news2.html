Ubisoft selected to publish Hostiles

Sep - 24 - 2019

We are extremely happy to announce that after meeting and talking with several game publishers over the last 3 months, we have choosen Ubisoft to be the publisher for our upcoming game Hostiles. The Danger Zone Game Studio president Mark Jones said "We really never thought that Ubisoft and other well established publishers would have approched us for this game. It was almost shocking to be honest". Hostiles is currently projected to be released on June 14th 2020.
